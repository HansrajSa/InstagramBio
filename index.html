Tips for Great Instagram Bios

Instagram Bio Best Practices

  • Keep it concise - 150 character limit
  • Include a call-to-action
  • Use line breaks for easier reading
  • Add relevant hashtags and emojis
  • Include your website or key link

Elements of a Perfect Bio

  • Your name and what you do
  • Your personality/brand vibe
  • Your interests or niche
  • Contact information
  • A unique hook to grab attention
